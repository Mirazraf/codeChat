# Privacy Settings User Guide

## Overview

CodeChat gives you complete control over your profile visibility. You can choose what information to share with other users through our flexible privacy system that works at both global and field levels.

## Understanding Privacy Levels

### Global Profile Visibility

Your **Global Visibility** setting acts as a master switch for your entire profile:

- **üîê Private (Default)**: Your profile is hidden from other users. Only your username, role, and avatar are visible.
- **üåê Public**: Your profile respects individual field privacy settings. Other users can see fields you've marked as public.

**Important**: When global visibility is set to private, all your profile information is hidden regardless of individual field settings.

### Field-Level Privacy

Each profile field has its own privacy control:

- **üîí Private**: The field is hidden from other users
- **üîì Public**: The field is visible to other users (only when global visibility is public)

## Managing Your Privacy Settings

### Changing Global Visibility

1. Navigate to your **Profile** page
2. Look for the **Global Visibility** toggle at the top
3. Click to switch between Private (üîê) and Public (üåê)
4. A confirmation message will appear

**Warning**: Setting your profile to private will hide all information from other users, regardless of individual field settings.

### Changing Field Privacy

1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Find the field you want to modify
3. Click the privacy icon next to the field:
   - üîí **Lock icon** = Currently private (click to make public)
   - üîì **Unlock icon** = Currently public (click to make private)
4. The change is saved automatically

### Viewing Your Privacy Status

On your profile page, you'll see:
- **Color coding**: Private fields have a red/orange tint, public fields have a green/blue tint
- **Icons**: Lock (üîí) for private, unlock (üîì) for public
- **Tooltips**: Hover over icons to see explanations
- **Privacy Summary**: A quick overview of all your privacy settings

## Viewing Other Users' Profiles

When you view another user's profile:

- If their **global visibility is private**: You'll only see username, role, and avatar
- If their **global visibility is public**: You'll see fields they've marked as public
- **Your own profile**: You always see all your fields with privacy indicators

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What happens when I set my profile to private?

**A**: Other users will only see your username, role, and avatar. All other information is hidden, regardless of individual field settings.

### Q: Can I make some fields public while keeping my profile private?

**A**: No. When global visibility is private, all fields are hidden. To share specific fields, set global visibility to public and then control individual fields.

### Q: Are my privacy settings saved permanently?

**A**: Yes. Your privacy settings are saved to your account and persist across sessions. They remain until you change them.

### Q: What are the default privacy settings for new users?

**A**: New users start with:
- Global visibility: Private
- All fields: Private (except avatar and bio, which are public)

This ensures maximum privacy from the start.

### Q: Can I see who viewed my profile?

**A**: Currently, CodeChat does not track profile views. Privacy settings control what others can see, but you won't know who viewed your profile.

### Q: Will my privacy settings affect my ability to use chat features?

**A**: No. Privacy settings only control your profile visibility. You can still participate in chats, send messages, and use all CodeChat features regardless of your privacy settings.

### Q: Can I export my privacy settings?

**A**: Privacy settings are part of your account data. Contact support if you need to export your complete account information.
